WikiSchools

Wikipedia articles for schools (2006).
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Wikipedia_for_Schools

4,403 nodes
112,834 edges in the graph of links
474,138 edges in the bipartie graph articles-words

Files:
* adjacency.tsv : list of edges (hypertext links article -> article)
* biadjacency.tsv : list of edges (article, word used in article, count)
* names.tsv : titles of articles
* names_col.tsv : words (stems)
* labels.tsv : labels (category of article)
* names_labels.tsv : names of labels (names of categories)
