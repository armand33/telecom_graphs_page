Wikilinks

Partial dump of Wikipedia (2013).
May 2020

3,210,346 nodes
67,196,296 edges in the graph of links
138,134,642 edges in the bipartie graph articles-stems

Files:
* adjacency.tsv : List of edges (article, article)
* biadjacency.tsv : List of edges (article, word used in summary, count)
* names.tsv : Titles of articles
* names_col.tsv : Words (stems)
* wikidata_ids.tsv : Corresponding identifiers for WikiData (see https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Wikidata:Main_Page)

Credits:
The link graph was obtained from https://snap.stanford.edu/data/enwiki-2013.html.
The stem biadjacency was obtained from https://github.com/tscheepers/Wikipedia-Summary-Dataset.
